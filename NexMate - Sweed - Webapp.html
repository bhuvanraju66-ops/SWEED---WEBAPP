Who We Are

SWEED Trust (Social Work & Economic Educational Development Trust) is a rural development NGO based in Tamil Nadu. Established to empower marginalized communities through education, livelihood, and social development programs.

Founded in 2009 by passionate social workers led by Mr. R. Selvaraju, SWEED has implemented numerous government and CSR-supported programs focusing on self-reliance and local capacity building.

  • Integrity and Transparency
  • Community Empowerment
  • Sustainability
  • Innovation in Rural Development
  • Inclusive Growth

SWEED operates under a registered trust model with statutory compliance under 12A, 80G, CSR-1, and DARPAN accreditation. Governance is ensured through regular board meetings and transparent financial reporting.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To foster socio-economic and educational empowerment of rural communities through participatory and sustainable development initiatives.

Vision: A self-reliant, equitable, and sustainable rural society where every individual has the opportunity to live with dignity.
